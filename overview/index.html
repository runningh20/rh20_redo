About RunningH20

The mission is to help companies, individuals, organizations, and entrepreneurs cross the digital divide by understanding and capitalizing on the technology of the Web. When entrepreneuralism is thwarted by one's geographical area, limited hyper-local or regional economy, legislative restrictions, et al, - RunningH20 helps them by using the Web to connect with otherwise out-of-reach markets, professional networks, supply chains, and knowledge-bases. We provide digital business tools (Websites, eCommerce websites, web/mobile apps, and digital advertising asset tools, as well as consultation on hardware technology to assist the online/connected digital tools and campaigns.

The main clientele that we are seeking is that which comes from geographically and economically dislocated areas (i.e. - crappy economies or locations that are disconnected to the large, economically thriving areas). In particular, we look to reach clientele in emerging markets typically located off-shore from the U.S., especially Brasil, Nigeria, Middle East states, Singapore and other Southeast Asian markets; which is why we're seeking multi-lingual, and culturally diverse/educated individuals.
